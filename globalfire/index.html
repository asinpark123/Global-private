Services

Some of the many services that we offer

Passive Fire Stopping

Penetrations through fire-rated assemblies, such as pipes, ducts, cables, and conduits, create pathways for fire and smoke to spread from one area of a building to another. Passive fire stopping measures involve sealing these penetrations with specified and industry tested fire-resistant materials to maintain the fire rating of the assembly and prevent the passage of fire and smoke.

Intumescent Painting

Intumescent painting is widely used in various types of buildings and structures, including commercial buildings, industrial facilities, and residential properties, where fire safety is a primary concern. By enhancing the fire resistance of structural elements, intumescent coatings help to improve overall building safety and protect occupants and assets in the event of a fire.

Fire Doors

Fire doors play a critical role in passive fire protection systems, helping to contain fires and limit their spread within buildings. Properly installed, maintained, and inspected fire doors are essential for ensuring the safety of occupants and minimising the impact of fires on property and assets.
