About Us

Global Linings are Specialists in Plasterboard Linings and Interior Solutions.

Established in 1991, Global Linings offer clients 30 years of trusted experience

Global Linings are the market leader for workmanship and project delivery

Universally Recognised Quality

Global Linings offer a top quality experienced team. From start to finish delivering exceptional quality plasterboard and interior finishing to projects big and small.

Services

Some of the many services that we offer

Plasterboard Linings

Global Linings provides a full service plasterboard lining solution including supply, fixing, and stopping of plasterboard for commercial and residential projects of any size

Painting

Global Linings can deliver a comprehensive painting solution inside and out

Steel Framing

Global Linings offers supply and install of direct fix suspended ceiling, two-way grid, steel stud and strapped walls

Insulation

Global Linings will also manage the installation of building insulation works, including underslab soffit liners

Barrierline, Weatherline & Other

Global Linings offer other installation solutions such as the Barrierline IT wall system, Weatherline, Fibre Cement Linings and more
